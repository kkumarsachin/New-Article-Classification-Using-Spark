SPORTSAn article on Thursday about the International Olympic Committee lifting its ban on Russia misstated the event in which Ekaterina Bobrova won a silver medal at the 2018 Winter Games. It was ice dancing, not pairs figure skating.THE ARTSBecause of an editing error, an article on Wednesday about the forthcoming exhibition “Heavenly Bodies: Fashion and the Catholic Imagination,” at the Costume Institute of the Metropolitan Museum of Art, misstated the location of the Chapel of the Rosary. It is in Vence, France, not Venice, Italy.•An article on Friday referred incorrectly to a Günther Förg retrospective at the Dallas Museum of Art. It will open this year, not in 2019.OPINIONAn Op-Ed essay on Wednesday about the race for the Democratic nomination for a House seat in Texas misstated the racial makeup of the slate of candidates. It is not the case that they all are white.OBITUARIESBecause of an editing error, an obituary on Wednesday referred incorrectly to Tony Conigliaro’s move to the California Angels in 1971.
